About the company

Intel is a global technology company established in 1968 and is recognized as one of the pioneers in computer hardware. It is best known for creating powerful processors that drive the majority of laptops and desktops around the world. From entry-level Celeron and Pentium chips to advanced Core and Xeon processors, Intel provides solutions for students, professionals, gamers, and creators. Many modern laptops also feature the Intel Evo platform, which ensures long battery life, fast charging, and smooth multitasking. With its reputation for speed, reliability, and innovation, Intel continues to be a leader in shaping the performance of laptops worldwide.

About the CEO

A CEO is the chief executive officer of a company, serving as its top leader and decision-maker. They oversee operations, set long-term goals, and create strategies for growth and success. A CEO motivates employees, builds strong relationships with stakeholders, and represents the organization publicly, ensuring innovation, efficiency, and accountability while guiding the business toward sustainable development and achievement.
